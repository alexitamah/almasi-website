Who We Are

Development-first.
Results that speak.

Almasi Football Academy is Abuja's premier youth football development programme. We identify raw talent from across West Africa and provide the coaching, competition, and professional infrastructure to turn potential into opportunity.

Our Mission

To identify, develop and elevate elite young footballers from West Africa, providing a genuine professional pathway to the global game.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted scout-facing development academy — where talent from Abuja to Banjul gets the visibility it deserves.

🌍

Our first international transfer is done. Almasi has already facilitated its first professional player signing — proof that the pipeline from Abuja to the global stage is real and working.

Why Almasi

The Data. The Standards.
The Results.

Scouts trust academies that operate like professional clubs. Here is how Almasi earns that trust.

📊

Data-Led Development

Every competitive match is filmed with Veo — the same video platform trusted by professional clubs across Europe. Scouts get real match footage, not edited show reels. Stats are sourced from Veo data and verified match reports.

🌍

West African Pipeline, Global Standards

Our players come from Nigeria, Gambia, Senegal, Ghana, and beyond. Our coaching methodology is built on international curricula, providing clubs with culturally adaptable players who hit the ground running.

✅

Scout-Ready Players

We understand what recruitment staff actually need: passport status, medical history, agent contacts, and fast communication. Our players are ready for immediate consideration — no admin friction, just talent.
